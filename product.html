About us:

Through three generations of family management, Kaffeparty Coffee has grown from a small, local coffee supplier to a well-respected leader in the office coffee service industry. It all began back in 1972, when the need for office coffee services was innovative and new to businesses.

Full Services:

Kaffeparty Coffee has been providing full-service office refreshment to businesses across Manhattan, Queens, Brooklyn, Long Island, New Jersey and Connecticut for more than 40 years.

Why us:

Are you passionate about gourmet coffee? So are we! In many ways, coffee gives us the inspiration we need to get through busy days and long nights. As a Kaffeparty Coffee franchisee, you can help inspire members of your community to achieve their daily goals while you work toward your own.
